Common Questions

The game does not open or crashes.

Restart your device, make sure you are using the latest app version, and try opening the game again. If the issue continues, email us with your device and app version.

I watched a rewarded ad but did not continue.

Rewarded ads require a completed ad view and a stable connection. If the reward is not granted, restart the game and try again. Contact us if the issue repeats.

Can I remove ads?

The current version may include rewarded and interstitial ads. If ad-removal or premium options are added in the future, they will be announced in the app or store listing.

Does the game require an account?

No. The current version does not require login, account creation, or a user profile.
