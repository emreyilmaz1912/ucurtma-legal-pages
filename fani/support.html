FAQ

My character died — can I bring them back?

No — in Fani, death is permanent; that is the game's one great rule. After death you may attempt the revenant trial or visit the memorial.

My purchase didn't unlock / was lost.

Try Settings → "Restore purchases". If it persists, email us with your purchase date.

I want my data deleted.

As described in our privacy policy, email us your character name and approximate date of death and we will delete the grave record.
