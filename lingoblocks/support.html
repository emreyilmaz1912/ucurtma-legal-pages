What to Include

To help us review your request faster, please include:

  • Your device model.
  • Your operating system version.
  • The app version if available.
  • A short description of the issue.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if it helps explain the issue.

Common Topics

  • Game progress or level issues
  • Word validation or missing words
  • Ads or rewarded ads
  • Remove Ads purchase or restore purchase
  • Crash or performance problems

Purchase Support

Purchases are processed by Google Play or Apple. If you purchased “Remove Ads” and it does not appear active, please try the restore purchases option in the app if available. If the issue continues, contact us with your device type and app version.
