LingoBlocks Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ucurtma (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information in the LingoBlocks mobile game.

1. App Information

App name: LingoBlocks
Package / Bundle ID: com.ucurtma.lingoblocks
Publisher: Ucurtma
Contact: medyaucurtma@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

LingoBlocks does not require users to create an account or log in. The game may process or store the following types of information:

  • Local gameplay data: level progress, coins, settings, language preference, daily reward state, and purchase state may be stored locally on your device.
  • Analytics data: app usage events, level progress events, crashes, diagnostics, device type, app version, and general performance data may be collected through Firebase services.
  • Advertising data: ads may be shown through Google AdMob. AdMob may process advertising identifiers and related data depending on your device settings and consent choices.
  • Purchase data: in-app purchase status such as “Remove Ads” may be processed through Google Play Billing or Apple StoreKit.
  • Optional word reports: if a missing-word reporting feature is enabled and you choose to report a word, the submitted word, language, level number, app version, platform, and timestamp may be sent for manual review.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Run core gameplay features.
  • Save your local progress and preferences.
  • Show ads and optional rewarded ads.
  • Process in-app purchases and restore purchases.
  • Understand crashes and improve app stability.
  • Improve dictionaries, levels, and game balance.

4. Third-Party Services

LingoBlocks may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Remote Config
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing
  • Apple StoreKit / In-App Purchases

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies and platform rules.

5. Advertising

LingoBlocks may show rewarded ads, interstitial ads, and limited banner ads. Rewarded ads are optional and provide in-game benefits when watched. If you purchase “Remove Ads”, forced ads such as interstitial and banner ads may be removed, while optional rewarded ads may remain available.

6. In-App Purchases

LingoBlocks may offer in-app purchases such as “Remove Ads”. Purchases are processed by Google Play or Apple, depending on your device. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.

7. Children’s Privacy

LingoBlocks is not specifically directed to children under the age required by applicable privacy laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and delete it if necessary.

8. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device unless you delete the app or reset progress. Analytics, crash, advertising, and optional report data may be retained by third-party services according to their own retention policies.

9. Your Choices

  • You can reset local game progress from the app if this option is available.
  • You can disable personalized ads from your device settings where supported.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove local data stored on your device.
  • You can contact us about privacy questions at medyaucurtma@gmail.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
