Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ucurtma (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information in the mobile game Grix Says Nope.

Grix Says Nope is a casual mobile game. It does not require account registration, login, user profiles, chat, or user-generated public content.

Information We Collect

We do not directly ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, address, or payment details inside the game.

The app may collect or process limited technical and usage information through third-party services used for analytics and advertising, including:

  • Device and app information, such as device type, operating system, app version, language, and country/region-level information.
  • Game usage events, such as game start, game over, rule changes, rewarded ad events, interstitial ad events, settings changes, and similar gameplay analytics.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data, when available and permitted by your device settings and applicable consent choices.
  • Diagnostics and performance information, such as crashes, errors, or basic performance signals if enabled through the services we use.

Third-Party Services

The game uses third-party services that may collect and process information according to their own policies:

  • Google AdMob — used to show rewarded and interstitial ads.
  • Firebase Analytics — used to understand app usage and improve the game.

How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the game.
  • Measure gameplay events and app performance.
  • Show ads, including rewarded ads and interstitial ads.
  • Prevent abuse, diagnose issues, and maintain app reliability.

Advertising and Consent

The game may show ads through Google AdMob. Depending on your location, device settings, and consent choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. You may be able to limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier from your device settings.

Where legally required, consent choices may be requested before using certain advertising or analytics features.

Children’s Privacy

Grix Says Nope is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by third-party service providers such as Google AdMob and Firebase to provide advertising, analytics, and app functionality.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by applicable law. Third-party providers may have their own retention periods.

Your Choices

  • You can adjust advertising identifier and tracking settings in your device settings.
  • You can delete the app to stop further collection by the app.
  • You can contact us with privacy questions or requests.

International Transfers

Third-party services may process information on servers located in different countries. By using the app, you understand that information may be processed outside your country of residence.
